FAQ

Good to know

How long does it take to edit a video?

Turnaround depends on scope — a simple social reel takes 2–3 days, while a brand film or documentary edit can take 1–3 weeks. You'll get a clear timeline before we start.

Can I be involved in the editing process?

Absolutely. I keep you in the loop with regular updates and revision rounds. Your feedback shapes the final product — collaborative, never secretive.

What formats do you accept for raw footage?

All major formats — MP4, MOV, ProRes, MXF, R3D, BRAW and more. Send what you have and I'll handle the rest.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on scope — quick reels, brand films and AI-generated ads all sit at different tiers. Book a call and you'll get a custom quote.

What if I'm not satisfied with the final edit?

Every project includes revision rounds. If you're not happy, I'll rework it until it meets your standard — your satisfaction is non-negotiable.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes — at least 2–3 revision rounds on every project, with more arranged based on the scope.
